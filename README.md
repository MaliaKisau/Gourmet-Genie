# 🧑‍🍳 Sassy Chef: Cooking Made Luxe  

Welcome to **Sassy Chef**, the Python app that serves you gourmet vibes with a dash of sass! Whether you're whipping up a quick snack or hosting a bougie dinner party, Sassy Chef has got you covered with luxe recipe ideas tailored to your mood and ingredients.  

---

## 🍽️ Features  
- **Mood-Based Recipes**: Feeling lazy? We got you. Feeling fancy? Let’s get extra.  
- **Ingredient Magic**: Input your pantry items, and Sassy Chef will whip up something fabulous.  
- **Sassy Output**: Not just recipes—expect a little attitude and a lot of fun!  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack  
- **Python**: Because simplicity is key.  
- **Random Library**: For generating creative recipe ideas.  
- **Text-Based Interface**: Luxe without the hassle of a GUI.  

---

## 🎉 How It Works  
1. **Choose Your Mood**: Tell Sassy Chef how you're feeling—lazy, fancy, or adventurous.  
2. **Provide Ingredients**: (Optional) Input what you have, and we'll work our magic.  
3. **Get Your Recipe**: Receive a sassy, luxe recipe suggestion tailored to you.
